    Is the new iPad Mini 6 2021 worth buying? ₹46900

    TABLET September 16, 2021

    Unlike the other products shown at the September 2021     Apple Event, the new iPad Mini 6 features a complete new Cutting Edge redesign. The newest iPad Mini is a major performance and camera improvement over the fifth generation when it comes to design and specs, which may have many fans thinking it’s time to upgrade. But stop do you know?, whether it’s worth buying or not.


    Is the new iPad Mini 6 2021 worth buying?


    The new iPad Mini 6 is a massive improvement over its predecessor. Given that it’s been two and a half years since the iPad Mini 5 was released, which was itself only a small improvement (visually) over the iPad Mini 4, a redesign was way overdue. Strangely, this 6th generation of the iPad Mini marks the first time the line has received a makeover, which brings it more in line with Apple’s iPad Pro series.

    Spec-wise, the iPad Mini 5 still isn’t too shabby. It shares the Apple A12 Bionic chip used in the iPhone XS line and has support for the first-generation Apple Pencil, along with a wide-color gamut display. However, it can’t compare to the new model, which launches with the Apple A15 Bionic process that powers the iPhone 13. The iPad Mini 6 outpaces its predecessor with a screen size increase (7.9″ to 8.3″), support for the Apple Pencil 2, and a switch to USB-C.


    Most importantly, the new iPad Mini 6 2021 doesn’t lose any functionality. At first glance, some users thought it might not have Touch ID, but this feature is not available through the power button on top of the tablet. Apple claims this iPad Mini has a 40 percent faster CPU and 80 percent faster GPU. So, even though the iPad Mini 5 is far from a slouch, this new device gives users a bit of future-proofing.

    For us, the choice of whether the new iPad Mini 6 2021 is worth it is an easy one. There’s no better tablet available in its size range, and it far surpasses previous models in every way.
